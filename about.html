Diana Țibre

Hi, I am Diana Țibre.

I am a young, self taught photographer, based in Cluj Napoca, Romania.
It all started in 2014 as a curiosity, but now it became passion. I believe that photographs are for being felt, for awakening that special emotion in your soul.
With my photographs I want to bring a little happiness in people's hearts. My greatest joy and my strongest motivation is when people admire my work.
I love shooting portraits, it is my strong point. I feel like candid and travel photography are the ones that represent me the most, because of the emotion they convey and its intensity. But I have always been curious to discover all branches of photography.
